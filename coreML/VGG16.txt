Model Name: VGG16

Description: Detects the dominant objects present in an image from a set of 1,000 categories such as trees, animals, food, vehicles, people, and more. 
The top-5 error from the original publication is 7.4%.

Core ML Model Size: 553.5 MB
<https://developer.apple.com/machine-learning/>
